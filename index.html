Welcome to the Huang Lab!

Located in the heart of Columbia University Irving Medical Center in New York City, we are a dedicated team of immunologists interested in uncovering novel cellular and molecular mechanisms governing immunology. Our research interests include but are not limited to mucosal immunology, cell migration, allergy, and themes of the innate immune system. Details on our current and published work can be found in the Research and Publication tabs, respectively. We are always looking to build fruitful collaborations and grow our lab, so please do not hesitate to reach out to us!
